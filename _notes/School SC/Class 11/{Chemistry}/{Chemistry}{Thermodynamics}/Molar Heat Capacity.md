- (C$_m$)
- Heat required to increase the temperature of one mole of substance by 1degree or 1 Kelvin.
- C$_m$ = $\frac{q}{n\Delta T}$
	- = > q = n C$_m$ $\Delta$T
- Unit J/ mol K
